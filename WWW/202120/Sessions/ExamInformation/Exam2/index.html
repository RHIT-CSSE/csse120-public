Exam 2: Description, How to Prepare, Admission Ticket, and How to Take the Exam Online

Exam 2 works just like Exam 1, except:

  • Your Hint Sheet for the Paper-and-Pencil part may be up to TWO 8.5 x 11 pages (or one double-sided page).

  • The Admission Ticket is to complete 15-Exam2Practice in PyCharm AND to do (and self-check) the Practice Paper-and-Pencil Problems.

    • See Session 14 of Moodle for those Practice Paper-and-Pencil problems and their Answer Key.

    • Session 14 of Moodle also has a sample paper-and-pencil exam and a recording of a Live Meeting in Microsoft Teams in which the instructor solved ALL of the problems in that sample paper-and-pencil exam.

      We strongly recommend that you attempt the problems in the sample paper-and-pencil exam and watch the recording of their solutions.

    • The 15-Exam2Practice in PyCharm has a module named optional_demo_practice_problems that is strongly recommended (but optional).

Note that, just like Exam 1, you may take the Exam wherever you want.

Note that, just like Exam 1, you should join the Live Meeting in Microsoft Teams shortly before 7:30 p.m. for any last-minute instructions. You will get an invitation to join that meeting, as usual.
