Exam 2: Description, How to Prepare, Admission Ticket, and How to Take the Exam Online

Exam 2 works just like Exam 1, except:

  • Your Hint Sheet for the Paper-and-Pencil part may be up to TWO 8.5 x 11 pages (or one double-sided page).

  • The Admission Ticket is to complete BOTH:

    • the 13-Exam2Practice (in PyCharm) on-the-computer practice problems, AND

    • the Practice Paper-and-Pencil Problems as described in 14-MoreExam2Practice (in PyCharm).

      • Write your answers to these problems, check your own answers and turn in your answers, all as described in 14-MoreExam2Practice (in PyCharm).

  • The 14-MoreExam2Practice has links to both required and optional (but highly recommended) practice problems, as well as answer keys to both.

Note that, just like Exam 1, you may take the Exam wherever you want.

Note that, just like Exam 1:

  • You take the On-the-Computer part of the exam in class.

    • Join the Video Class Meeting at the beginning of class as usual for instructions for starting that part of the exam.

  • You take the Paper-and-Pencil part of the exam at a time of your own choosing on or before Tuesday, April 20, 11:59 p.m.
